## ü™∑ Understanding the System

### The Sacred Structure

The Parva system is built on the power of **7** - a number representing completeness and cycles in many traditions.

**343 Days = 7 √ó 7 √ó 7**

#### Level 1: Maha-Parva (343 days)
The grand journey. Nearly a year of dedicated practice.

#### Level 2: Parva (49 days each, 7 total)
Each Parva has one of seven themes:
1. **Beginning** (Violet) - Laying the foundation
2. **Practice** (Indigo) - Building through repetition
3. **Discernment** (Blue) - Developing clarity
4. **Ascent** (Green) - Accelerating growth
5. **Mastery** (Yellow) - Solidifying skills
6. **Flow** (Orange) - Finding rhythm
7. **Renewal** (Red) - Integration and rest

#### Level 3: Saptaha (7 days each, 7 per Parva)
A Saptaha is a "week" that also follows the same 7 themes.

Within the "Beginning" Parva, you'll have:
- Saptaha 1: Beginning within Beginning
- Saptaha 2: Practice within Beginning
- Saptaha 3: Discernment within Beginning
- ...and so on

This creates a beautiful fractal pattern!

#### Level 4: Dina (1 day, 7 per Saptaha)
Each day has its own micro-theme:
1. **Initiate** - Start fresh
2. **Stabilize** - Build consistency
3. **Observe** - Notice patterns
4. **Strengthen** - Push through
5. **Expand** - Go further
6. **Integrate** - Connect the dots
7. **Reflect** - Look back and learn

These themes repeat every 7 days throughout the entire 343-day cycle.

## üì± Using the App

### Home Screen

**What you see:**
- List of all your Maha-Parvas
- Current day and progress for active ones
- + button to create new Maha-Parva

**Actions:**
- **Tap a card** ‚Üí View the 7-petal mandala
- **Tap +** ‚Üí Create new 343-day cycle
- **Tap ‚ãÆ menu** ‚Üí Open settings

### Creating Your First Maha-Parva

1. Tap the **+** floating button
2. Enter a meaningful title (e.g., "Spiritual Practice", "Learn Programming")
3. Add a description (optional but recommended)
4. Set start date (defaults to today)
5. Add accountability partner email (optional)
6. Save

**The app automatically generates all 343 days with proper themes!**

### The Mandala View

When you tap a Maha-Parva, you see a beautiful circular mandala divided into 7 colored sections.

**Features:**
- Each petal represents one Parva (49 days)
- Colors follow VIBGYOR (Violet ‚Üí Red)
- Current active Parva is highlighted
- Center shows your current position

**Navigation:**
- Tap any petal ‚Üí Drill into that Parva's 49 days

### Parva Detail (49 Days)

**Two view modes:**

#### List View (Default)
- 7 cards showing each Saptaha
- Color-coded by theme
- Shows date ranges
- "Active" badge on current Saptaha

#### Mandala View
- Another 7-section circular view
- Same color scheme
- Interactive sections

**Change view mode in Settings**

### Saptaha Detail (7 Days)

Shows all 7 Dinas (days) in the week:
- Day number (1-343 overall)
- Dina theme name
- Date
- "Today" badge if applicable
- Preview of notes (if any)
- ‚úì checkmark if completed

**Tap any day** ‚Üí Open Dina detail

### Dina Detail (Single Day)

**The heart of your practice!**

**Information shown:**
- Day X of 343
- Dina theme (Initiate/Stabilize/etc.)
- Full date
- Current Parva and Saptaha context

**Actions:**
1. **Write Notes** - Your daily journal entry
2. **Mark Complete** - Toggle completion status
3. **Save** - Persist your notes

**Your notes are timestamped for posterity!**

## üí° Tips for Success

### 1. Daily Ritual
Make checking your Dina part of your morning or evening routine.

### 2. Write Something Every Day
Even a single sentence. It's powerful to look back months later.

### 3. Use the Themes as Prompts
If stuck, reflect on:
- How did today's Dina theme show up?
- How does the current Saptaha theme feel?
- What's the Parva teaching me?

### 4. Review Weekly
Every Saptaha 7 (Reflect), read your week's notes.

### 5. Celebrate Transitions
When moving to a new Parva, acknowledge the shift in energy.

### 6. Be Gentle with Gaps
Missed days? That's okay. Pick up where you are, not where you "should" be.

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### App won't start
- Ensure Android 7.0+ (API 24+)
- Clear cache: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Parva ‚Üí Clear Cache

### Notifications not working
- Check Settings ‚Üí Parva ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Allow
- Battery optimization might block: Exempt Parva from restrictions

### Journal export not working
- Grant file permissions if asked
- Ensure email app is installed

### Mandala not displaying
- Restart app
- Check for app updates
